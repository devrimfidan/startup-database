About Cambridge Startups Directory

Last Updated: June 3, 2025

Cambridge Startups Directory is a comprehensive tool designed to help users discover and explore innovative companies based in Cambridge and beyond using official Companies House data.

Our Mission

We aim to make corporate information more accessible and user-friendly, enabling entrepreneurs, investors, researchers, and the general public to gain valuable insights into the Cambridge business ecosystem.

Cambridge has one of the most dynamic startup ecosystems in Europe, with strengths in technology, biotech, AI, and clean energy. Our directory helps you navigate this exciting landscape.

How It Works

Cambridge Startups Directory connects directly to the Companies House API to provide you with up-to-date information on registered companies. Here's how to use our service:

  1. Get an API Key - Register for a free API key from the Companies House Developer Portal
  2. Enter Your Key - Your key is stored securely in your browser session only
  3. Search - Use keywords, company names, or locations to find relevant businesses
  4. Filter & Sort - Refine results by company age, status, type, and more
  5. Explore - View detailed information about companies that interest you

About the Data

All company information is sourced directly from Companies House, the United Kingdom's registrar of companies. The data is publicly available and provided under the UK Open Government Licence.

Please note that while we strive for accuracy, we cannot guarantee the completeness or timeliness of the data. Always verify critical information through official channels.
