Privacy Policy

Cambridge Startups Directory

Last Updated: June 3, 2025
Privacy Summary: We collect minimal personal data, do not store your API keys, and only display publicly available company information from Companies House.

1. Information We Collect

Cambridge Startups Directory collects minimal information to provide our service:

  • Companies House API Key: Temporarily stored in your browser session only
  • Search Queries: Not stored or logged
  • Basic Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (page views, popular searches)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information for security and performance

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Facilitate API calls to Companies House on your behalf
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Ensure website security and prevent abuse
  • Generate anonymous usage statistics

We do not: Sell your data, share it with third parties, or use it for marketing purposes.

3. API Key Handling

Important: Your Companies House API key is never stored on our servers.

Your API key is:

  • Stored only in your browser's memory during your session
  • Used exclusively to make API calls to Companies House
  • Deleted when you close your browser or navigate away
  • Never transmitted to our servers or stored in any database

4. Data Sources

All company information displayed comes from:

  • Companies House API: Publicly available UK company data
  • This data is already public and regulated by Companies House privacy policies
  • We do not add, modify, or enhance this data with additional personal information

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Our website uses minimal tracking:

  • No persistent cookies for user tracking
  • Session storage only for your API key during use
  • Essential cookies only for basic website functionality
  • No third-party advertising or tracking cookies

6. Third-Party Services

We interact with the following external services:

  • Companies House API: Subject to their privacy policy and terms
  • GitHub Pages: Hosting service (if applicable)
  • No social media integrations or advertising networks

7. Your Rights (GDPR/UK GDPR)

Under data protection law, you have rights including:

  • Right to be informed: This privacy policy explains our practices
  • Right of access: Request copies of any personal data we hold
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a usable format
  • Right to object: Object to processing of your personal data

Note: Since we store minimal data, most requests will result in confirmation that we hold no personal data about you.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • No server-side storage of sensitive information
  • Regular security reviews and updates
  • Minimal data collection principle

9. Data Retention

We retain data for minimal periods:

  • API Keys: Not stored (session only)
  • Search queries: Not logged or stored
  • Analytics data: Anonymized, retained for 12 months maximum
  • Server logs: Retained for 30 days for security purposes

10. International Data Transfers

Data processing occurs within the UK/EU. If data is transferred internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place including adequacy decisions or standard contractual clauses.

11. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
